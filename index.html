About My Work

As an embedded systems engineer, I am passionate about creating the foundational technology that powers tomorrow's innovations. My expertise spans low-level firmware development, custom circuit design, and system-level integration. I'm adept at working with various microcontrollers, from tiny 8-bit devices to powerful ARM processors, and I have extensive experience in real-time operating systems (RTOS), communication protocols (I2C, SPI, UART), and driver development.

I thrive on solving complex technical challenges and providing clean, well-documented, and scalable code. My goal is to deliver a solution that is not only functional but also maintainable and ready for mass production.
